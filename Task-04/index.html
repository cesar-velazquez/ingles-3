Recap of Module 4. I have just moved to another city

In this module…

You learned the present perfect tense to talk about life experiences. You require the verb have as an auxiliary verb and the main verb in past participle form:

  • I have ridden a motorcycle on the beach.
  • My mother has been to different countries in her life.

You can use some adverbs and prepositions with the present perfect tense such as already, yet, ever, never, just, for  and since: 

  • Tom has studied French for 3 years. / Tom has studied French since he was in high school.
  • I have already finished university. / I haven’t finished university yet.
  • Have you ever driven a racing car?
  • They have just fallen in love.

PLT04B. I have just moved to another city…

Instructions:

  1. Write a text (paragraph 100 - 130 words), describing some of your life experiences. Title your text “What I have done in my life up to now”. Use the structures and vocabulary of this module and pay attention to the spelling, punctuation and use of capital letters. This text will be your script for an audio you will record. Look at the example below.

    2. These are some ideas you can include and write about in your text: 

    • A dream that you have had and later came true.
    • A famous person you have met.
    • A foreign country you have travelled to.
    • Something disgusting you have eaten.
    • Something you have won (For example, the lottery, a sport competition, a contest, etc.).
    • A frightening experience you have had when travelling (on a bus, plane, bike, etc.).
    • The best and worst movies you have ever seen. 
    • A city you have visited more than once.
    • A concert you have recently been to.
  2. Read your script and practice your pronunciation.
  3. Record your script. Communicate your ideas clearly, with correct pronunciation and the necessary pauses between ideas.
  4. Create an MP3 file and name it: PLT04_ last name and name (Example: PLT04_SanchezJorge).
  5. Copy and paste your text (script) in the Word processor space in your course in the Activities section module 4. (Look at the example below)
  6. Upload your MP3 file in your course in the Activities section module 4, or share the link of your drive (GOOGLE DRIVE, ONEDRIVE, etc.). Be sure the link is open to anyone.

Evidences of the task:

  • A text (script): “What I have done in my life up to now”.
  • A recording of this text: MP3 file.

Example of script:

What I have done in my life up to now.

I’ve trained hard in my sport: athletics, and I have developed great abilities. I’ve learned to jump long distances and to run very fast. I’ve taken part of the Panamerican Games and have won a lot of medals. I’ve been successful as a solo runner. I’ve broken many world records. Last games I broke one at short distances. For this reason, I’ve travelled to many countries in Europe as well as in Central and South America. I’ve been to Italy, Spain, Brazil and Peru, but I haven’t been to Argentina yet. In Peru, I visited the ruins of Machu Picchu and in Italy I went to Rome. I have been there more than once in my life. (129 words).
