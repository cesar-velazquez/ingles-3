Module 3. I was telling a story when...

Welcome to the module

Listen to the audio.

In this module, you will describe actions or states that happened in the past, and whose duration is relevant. You will compare them with those that were in progress at specific time in the past by using while y when. Besides, you will identify sequence words in stories.

Good luck!

Topics to talk about

What were you doing?
Past continuous: affirmative, negative and interrogative
I was… while you were…
Past continuous vs. past simple WHEN y WHILE
They say that...
Sequence words for stories
