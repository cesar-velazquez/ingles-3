There are regular an irregular verbs in past. The regular verbs are added an “ed” to form their past tense: Play-played.

The irregular verbs change their spelling: “go- went”. We also use the auxiliary “did” for the negative and interrogative form.

  • They didn't pay the telephone bill.
  • Did you eat vegetables this week?
