Recap of Topic 4.1 I have studied…

Recall what you learned:

There are regular and irregular verbs.

Regular verbs only add -ed to the end to make the past simple and past participle form. Examples:

  • To travel – travelled – travelled
  • To start – started - started

However, some other verbs do not have regular form and it is necessary to pay close attention to their past simple and past participle form because they are irregular. Examples:

  • To drive – drove – driven
  • To sleep – slept - slept
