Guidelines

  1. Prepare the timeline using the software or platform that suits you.
  2. On your timeline, you should describe the most important events in your life.
  3. It is suggested to start with your birth and then describe some of your celebrations, your first days of school, your graduations, family events, among others and end with the most important thing that has happened to you this year 2024.
  4. You must include (10 - 12) at least 10 important events.
  5. The description of each event must include:
    • Dates: Mainly the year of the event.
    • Descriptions: Describe the event in simple past.
    • Headers: Title of the event. Example: Birth.
    • Images (optional): They add graphic interest and additional information.
  6. If you wish, you can include animations.
  7. Submit your final project on the date indicated by your facilitator.
