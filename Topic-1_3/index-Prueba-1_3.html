So how do you describe a person?

There are two ways to describe people in English. You can describe people’s appearance and personality.

Appearance

To describe a person’s appearance in English, you can use adjectives to describe their physical characteristics such as their height, build, hair and facial features.

When you ask about someone’s physical appearance you need to use the following questions:
