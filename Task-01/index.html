Platform learning task for Module 1. My habits and hobbies

Objective: To review the structures and vocabulary seen in this module.

Instructions:

  1. Choose a famous family in the world.
  2. Find some pictures which depict this family.
  3. Write a 120–150-word description of this family. Begin by saying who they are, what’s the relationship among them. Then, describe each one’s physical appearance. Finally, say what they do or don’t do every day.
  4. Make sure your text is clear regarding the structures, spelling and punctuation.
  5. Once your text is ready, record a short video in which you introduce that family.
  6. Practice so that your voice is clear.
  7. Make sure your video is open and can be seen in any device.
  8. Upload both your text and video in the corresponding place in the platform
  9. In case your video exceeds the limit allowed, upload it to Drive, Dropbox or YouTube and share the link in the space provided for this activity. Make sure it has no restrictions.
  10. Pay attention to the rubric so that you can get all points.
