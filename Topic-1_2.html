Online Practice Activity
OPA06. My lovely family

Instructions:
1. Read the following text.

My lovely family

Hi! I’m Jenny, I’m 16 years old and this is my family. We’re from New York city but we live in London. My mother is a teacher, her name is Hayley, she is 38 years old. My father is an engineer, he is 40 years old, his name’s Nick. I have two older brothers Tony and Peter. Tony is 24 years old; he is married to a Brazilian woman but they live in Canada. They have two kids, Mary, and John. Peter is two years younger than Tony and he lives in Mexico with my grandparents, my aunt, my uncle, and my cousins because he is studying at the university. He wants to be a doctor.

On vacation we always visit my family in Mexico. I like to go to the beach and play volleyball with Rita and Sandy, my aunt’s daughters, they are 16 too. We enjoy being on the beach. Rita can swim but Sandy is a better swimmer, I can’t swim very well, but I enjoy being with them. In the afternoons, my two brothers sometimes play chess or video games in the living room while my parents prepare some delicious food with my aunt and uncle. We really enjoy our vacation.

2. Choose if the following staments are True or False.

a. Jenny’s parents are British.

b. Nick’s son is Peter.

c. Mary is Tony’s wife.

d. Peter is John’s uncle.

e. Peter is 22 years old.

f. Rita and Sandy are Mary’s cousins.

Very good! You are ready to continue.

Please, try to answer the exercise again.

Please, check the correct answers.

Jenny’s parents are British is False. Nick’s son is Peter is True. Mary is Tony’s wife is False. Peter is John’s uncle is True. Peter is 22 years old is True. Rita and Sandy are Mary’s cousins is True.

3. Click on the buttons and check your answers.
